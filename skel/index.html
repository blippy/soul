FAQ

Q01 How can I get my link added to the list?

People sometimes attempt to add URLs to my summary links by replying to my original "alt.comp.freeware links at NNN" postings. This is not a good idea, because it means that your URL will be categorised under the heading of "alt.comp.freeware links ...", rather than a heading that is more meaningful for your URL.

Your best bet (it really is better for you!) is simply to post a normal message to the alt.comp.freeware group giving a meaningful subject line and body message. Don't forget to give the URL that you want people to go to. SOUL identifies URLs as those beginning with www or http, so something like 

    www.markcarter.me.uk
or 
    http://www.markcarter.me.uk
will do just as well. SOUL will automatically read your message when it does its batch run and summarise it for you. Simple!

Q02 Shouldn't your list eliminate spam/off-topic content?

Ideally, yes. In reality, SOUL would have to be much more sophisticated, and would require a programming effort that I am not prepared to make. The links are generated automatically by the SOUL robot, and it has absolutely no way of distinguishing between genuine freeware and pr0n, malware, and suchlike. SOUL has sources which are in the public domain, so feel free to enhance it how you wish. If you get something really super going, then I'd be happy to hand over the reins to you.
